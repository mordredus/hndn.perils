What is HN-DN?

HN-DN (pronunciation: Hundun) is a five-member multinational group formed under APL LABELS in 2023.

The group consists of members from the Philippines, United States, Indonesia, South Korea, and the United Kingdom.

Concept

HN-DN’s artistic identity centers around existentialism.

Their music explores chaos, identity, consciousness, rebirth, and self-determination.

The Meaning Behind Their Name

HN-DN derives from Hun Dun — the primordial chaos in Chinese philosophy and mythology.

It represents formless existence before structure, and the beauty of undefined identity.

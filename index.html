WHAT IS HN-DN?

HN-DN (pronounced Hundun) is a five-member group formed under APL LABELS in 2023. The members represent the Philippines, United States, Indonesia, South Korea, and the United Kingdom.

CONCEPT

HN-DN’s artistic identity revolves around existentialism — questioning identity, reality, meaning, chaos, and consciousness.

MEANING

The name originates from Hun Dun, the concept of primordial chaos in Chinese philosophy and mythology.

What is HN-DN?

HN-DN (pronunciation: Hundun) is a five-member group formed under APL LABELS in 2023. The group consists of members from the Philippines, United States, Indonesia, South Korea, and the United Kingdom.

Concept

Their concept centers around Existentialism, exploring the human condition, chaos, and self-awareness through music.

Meaning Behind the Name

HN-DN comes from Hun Dun, the concept of primordial chaos in Chinese philosophy and mythology.
